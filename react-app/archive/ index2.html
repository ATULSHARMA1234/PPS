Prakash Parcel Services Limited

Your Preferred Logistics Partner!

Prakash Parcel Services Limited started in 1992, was the parcel division of the erstwhile Prakash Group, one of India's few transport companies started in the 1960s. Over the years, PPS has transformed into a renowned logistics service provider working with several corporate houses, MNCs, MSMEs & PSUs supporting their physical distribution across India. We at PPS believe that we are partners in the growth of our customers.

Our Management has over 5 decades of industry knowledge and our operations team comprises well experienced members, thus we assure best services to our customers. Our emphasis on quality & technology helps maintain repeatability in our services.

Our network is spread across 19 states covering all major cities & industrial towns.
