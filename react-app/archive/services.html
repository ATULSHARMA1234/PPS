Our Services

Comprehensive Logistics Solutions for Your Business

PPS Logistics offers a wide range of logistics and supply chain solutions tailored to meet the unique needs of businesses across industries.

Full Truck Load (FTL)

Our FTL service provides exclusive use of an entire truck for your shipment, ensuring direct routes without any transshipment delays or risks.

  • Direct point-to-point delivery
  • Zero transshipment handling
  • Scheduled pickup and delivery
  • Dedicated vehicle assignment
  • Real-time tracking & updates

Partial Truck Load (PTL)

For shipments that don't require a full truck, our PTL service optimizes costs while maintaining speed and reliability through our consolidated network.

  • Cost-effective shipping
  • Frequent service frequency
  • Safe cargo consolidation
  • Flexible pickup & delivery
  • Pan-India network coverage

Warehousing & Distribution

Our secure warehousing solutions provide strategic storage and distribution centers across major hubs with advanced inventory management systems.
